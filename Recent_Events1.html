Honiton Fight Night - 14th November 2009

On the 14th of November, back in 2009, three Full Circle members took part in the Honiton Fight Night with great results.

Up for the Kickboxing bouts were Chris Bladon, Owen Edwards and Conor Crutchley.

Chris Bladon, an instructor from Full Circle, fought terrifically and managed to win his fight.

Owen Edwards weighed in too heavy, however, the fight was declared to be an exhibition match where no matter what happened, the fight would result in a draw, but still Owen fought magnificently, especially as a fighter aged only 16 fighting against someone aged 23.

Conor Crutchley was in for a title fight where the winner would be declared South West Champion. With this being only his second fight, it was quite an achievement to even be considered for a title shot, but nevertheless, he went through with it. Conor was matched up with 21 year old Matt Wales and knowing that his opponent was 5 years older than him, he still went on with the 4 round title fight. Unfortunately, Conor lost his fight due to T.K.O. but he still performed very well for what was only his second fight.
